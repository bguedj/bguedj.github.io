Translator

I mainly use [DeepL](https://www.deepl.com/translator) for quick translation. Success story from 2018: with a bunch of colleagues from France, we translated the book ["Deep Learning"](http://www.deeplearningbook.org) by Goodfellow, Bengio and Courville to French in a couple months: introducing [l'Apprentissage profond](https://www.apprentissageprofond.org). Our secret? Scripts to massively accelerate translation with [DeepL](https://www.deepl.com/translator).
