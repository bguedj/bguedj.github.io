Translator

I mainly use DeepL for quick translation. Success story from 2018: with a bunch of colleagues from France, we translated the book "Deep Learning" by Goodfellow, Bengio and Courville to French in a couple months: introducing "L'Apprentissage profond". Our secret? Scripts to massively accelerate translation with DeepL.
